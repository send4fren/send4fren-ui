Instruction

Make sure both you and your fren has a Phantom Wallet.



Get Solana (SOL) from centralised exchanges (Coinbase, Binance, Crypto.com, FTX) and send it to your Phantom Wallet address.



Go through the minting process, select the card theme you want and decide if you want to send4fren for send4me




If you want to send4fren, get their wallet address and paste it on the "solana address" bar, you won’t need to do that if it's for yourself.




Once your card has been successfully minted, a little party will show along with your photo! P.S. make sure to tell your fren to check their wallet for their surprise :)
