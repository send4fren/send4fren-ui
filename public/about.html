about us

NFTs have been traditionally locked at high prices making it inaccessible for the general public to gift each other. We are making NFT gift giving affordable for everyone. We create celebration card for every big occasion to make gift giving easy when you don't know what to get someone.

Each NFT is created and programmatically generated, each component has been designed by the team to ensure a high-quality celebration card is received.
